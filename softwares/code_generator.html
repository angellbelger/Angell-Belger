Federal Law 13,675/18 of Brazil, outlined in Article 43, stipulates that "functional identification documents of professionals in the field of public security and social defense will be standardized by the decree of the Extraordinary Minister of State for Public Security, possessing public faith and validity across the entire national territory."

Before this regulation, each entity within the Single Public Security System bore full responsibility for determining the specifications of their respective functional identities. Mr. Belger, amid limited institutional resources, undertook the pivotal role of resolving this task. He played a key role in both the graphic and technological aspects of creating a document that could be universally recognized and valid throughout the country.
