Data-Criminis

The Data-Criminis system operates as an intranet within the SEGMUC system (Municipal Secretariat for Public Security, Urban Mobility, and Citizenship Articulation), both of which were developed by Mr. Belger. This project stands out as the most significant to date. In addition to establishing a comprehensive database containing information about all agents, it played a crucial role in phasing out the use of paper records during crises.

Furthermore, the system has undergone continuous development, introducing valuable features over time. These include the creation and management of police reports, a QR code-based validation system for functional identity within the system, and the ability to generate insightful statistics through detailed reports. This multifaceted approach has not only streamlined data management but has also enhanced the overall efficiency of public security operations.
